2 Procedures

The first wave of this study will include recruitment of 30 high school students (ages 14-18) to take part in an intervention to improve body image and advocate for body positivity. Participants will complete four facilitator-lead, 1-hour sessions, and complete a series of assessments at baseline, post-intervention, and at the conclusion of the final session and 1-month follow-up.
